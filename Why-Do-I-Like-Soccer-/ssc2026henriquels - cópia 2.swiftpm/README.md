# About
Why Do I Like Soccer? is an app that explores the emotional and strategic sides of soccer through personal storytelling and an interactive tactical experience. Position your attacking players on the field, receive animated feedback on your formation, and discover why soccer demands not just passion, but real intelligence.


# Run Device
The app was designed to run on Apple iPad 11-inch in landscape orintation, on the Xcode simulator or physical device.
